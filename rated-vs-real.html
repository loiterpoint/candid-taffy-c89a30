// How to read this

We don't run our own lab, and we say so. Every measured figure here is drawn from published independent testing or verified owner reports, and is cited in the full review linked on each row. Where a source reported a range, we show the range rather than the flattering end of it.

A gap is not automatically a scandal. Battery ratings are measured at fixed volume or with features disabled, and solar output is rated at panel-face irradiance you will not see on a cloudy afternoon. The point isn't that manufacturers lie — it's that the rated number and the number you'll live with are different, and only one of them is printed on the box.

Found a figure we got wrong, or newer testing that supersedes ours? That's a correction we want — our scores move when the evidence does.
