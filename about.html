How we score

Every review uses the same methodology: we aggregate measured results from independent testing — wind handling, range as flown, battery to actual cutoff rather than the manufacturer's optimistic quoted runtime — and cross-check them against owner-reported data and our own analysis of firmware changelogs. Where the evidence is thin or conflicting, we say so rather than inventing certainty. Scores are out of 10 to one decimal place and represent our genuine assessment. An 8.5 is not "pretty good" — it means something specific keeps it from a 9.

Our editorial principles

No sponsored reviews. Ever.
We have never accepted payment, free product, or early access in exchange for a favorable review. Brands can send us review units — if they do, we say so. Our score is unaffected.
Affiliate links are disclosed, not hidden.
This site participates in the Amazon Associates program. When you buy through our links we earn a small commission at no cost to you. Every affiliate link is labeled. It does not influence what we recommend or how we score anything.
Firmware changes matter. We track them.
DJI and Autel quietly change flight characteristics, obstacle avoidance behavior, and video processing in firmware updates. We compare changelogs against owner-reported behavior after significant updates and document what actually changed — not just what the changelog says.
We cover FPV and building, not just consumer drones.
Anyone who flies knows that the consumer and FPV worlds overlap. We cover Betaflight tuning, FPV build guides, and component reviews for pilots who build their own aircraft.
